Pork Adobo

Description

Pork adobo is a dish where pork is being simmered slowly with soy sauce.

This is perfect with rice and also it has slight sweetness to balance out the saltiness of the soy sauce.

I recommend to use pork belly for maximum flavor.

Ingredients

  • Pork belly
  • Garlic
  • Dried bay leaves
  • White vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Whole peppercorns
  • Water
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Marinate the pork with soy sauce and garlic in a bowl
  2. Sear the pork over medium heat until it goes brown
  3. Simmer the pork with water and aromatics with low heat
  4. Finish with vinegar and sugar for your preferred sweetness
  5. Serve hot over rice and enjoy!
